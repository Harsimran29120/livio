    About Me

    Education

    University of Milan (Department of Oriental Language Culture)

    Education

    Siena Jazz University (Piano Jazz and Composition)

    I was born in Gallarate, Italy on May 18, 1988 and began playing the piano in 1999 at age 11. I participated in several classical and jazz music masterclasses with: Gloria Campaner, Irene Veneziano, Dave Liebman, Joe Chambers, Ron Carter, Logan Richardson, Neil Angilley, Luciano Biondini, Greg Cohen, Paolo Fresu,Tullio de Piscopo, Horarcio Hernandez, Marcus Miller, Chucho Valdès, Naima Acuña, Marcus Gilmore, Ben Wendel, Kenny Werner, Gerard Clayton, Matt Mitchell, Yonathan Avishai, Stefano Battaglia, and Siena Jazz Summer Workshop playing with Claudio Fasoli, Drew Gress, and Lage Lund

    Experience

    • 2002

    Began playing his piano repertoire in various theaters, restaurants, hotels and open air concerts within the Lombardy region of Italy.

    • 2003

    Won 1st place playing Aram Kachaturian-Toccata in E flat minor at the Stresa International Music Competition, score: 100/100.

    • 2004

    Won 1st place playing Chopin-Fantaisie Impromptu op. 66 at the Stresa International Music Competition, score: 95/100.


    Selected from hundreds as one of the top 12 best young pianists (contestants ranged from age 12-19) in Lombardy, in the “MUSICA IN LUCE" competition.


    Played Rachmaninov-Prelude in C sharp minor and Rimsky Korsakov-Flight of the Bumblebee, in the final concert at ‘’TEATRO DAL VERME’’ in Milan for the final performance.

    • 2005

    Began his musical composing journey and won 2nd place playing his first piece called “BRIGHT CLOUD” at the Stresa International Music Competition, score: 92/100.

    • 2007

    Won 1st place playing the Aram Khachaturian-Saber Dance at the Omegna music competition, score: 96/100.

    • 2008

    Developed an even deeper interest in the electronic music scene of the early years of the century which led him to start a non-profit project, creating the ‘’VOICE UNDERGROUND’’ label with the collaboration of two DJs / producers: Funder (aka Federico Ricchiuto) and DJ Fonta (aka Luca Fontana).

    • 2011

    Played a four-hand piano performance with the pianist Christian Salerno in a youtube video that got 10,000+ views: watch here
